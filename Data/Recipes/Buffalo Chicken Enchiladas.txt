INGREDIENTS
A stack of tortillas
2 cups of Buffalo Sauce
3 bowls of shredded chicken
a wedge of Monterry Jack Cheese
sour cream
chives
INSTRUCTIONS
Heat up the tortillas on a pan 
Dip the heated tortillas in the buffalo sauce 
Lay each buffalo dipped tortilla on a plate in order to place the shredded chicken
Roll the tortilla so no chicken will fall out
shred the cheese over the desired amount of enchiladas made 
cook the enchiladas in a pan on a stove to melt the cheese
add sour cream on the top
add chives on the top
