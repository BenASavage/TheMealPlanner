INGREDIENTS
1 pound of small red-skinned potatoes
4 tablespoons of unsaltest butter
4 5-ounce skin-on center-cut salmon fillets
1/4 teaspoon of paprika
1/2 teaspoon of gated lemon zest
4 cloves garlic 
INSTRUCTIONS
put the potatoes in the bottom of an instant pot
add 1 cup of water, 2 tablespoons of butter, 1/2 teaspoon of salt
place the pots steam rack over the potatoes
rub the top and sides of the salmon fillets with the paprika and lemon zest
place skin-side down on the rack
put on the lid and set cooker to high pressure for 3 minutes
remove the salmon and the rack and set the cooker to saute at normal heat
when the potatoes beggin to soften, add garlic and cook up to 1 to 2 miniutes
Turn off the cooker
season the potatoes with salt and pepper
divide the salmon and potato mixture among plates
Enjoy!
