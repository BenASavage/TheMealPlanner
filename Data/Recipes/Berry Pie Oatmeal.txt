INGREDIENTS
1/2 cup rolled oats
1/2 cup milk or almond milk
1/2 cup water
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
Pinch of salt
1/2 cup blueberries, divided
1 teaspoon butter
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
2 tablespoons vanilla Greek yogurt
2 teaspoons maple syrup
INSTRUCTIONS
In a medium microwave-safe bowl, add oats, milk, water, vanilla, and salt. Stir to combine.
Microwave for about 3 minutes, or until oats are cooked through.
When oats are cooked, add half the blueberries (1/4 cup), butter, and cinnamon. Stir to combine.
Top oatmeal with yogurt and remaining blueberries. Drizzle maple syrup on top.
For extra sweetness, you can add 1 tablespoon brown sugar or 2-4 tablespoons applesauce.
