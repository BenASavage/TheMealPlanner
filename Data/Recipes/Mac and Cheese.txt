INGREDIENTS
4 cups dried macaroni
1 whole egg
1/2 stick of butter
1/4 cup of all purpose flour
2 1/2 cups of whole milk
2 teaspoons of dry mustard
1 pound shrp cheddar
salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
INSTRUCTIONS
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
Cook the macaroni until still slightly firm
drain and set aside
beat the egg
melt the butter and sprinkle the flour
whisk the egg and butter mixture over medium-low heat
cook for a couple of minutes, whisk constantly
pour the milk, add the mustard and whisk until smooth
cook until very thick for about 5 minutes
reduce the heat to low
add in the cheese and stir to melt
add 1/2 teaspoon salt and 1/2 pepper
pour in the drained, cooked macaroni and stir to combine
Enjoy!
