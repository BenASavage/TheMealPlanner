INGREDIENTS
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup baking cocoa
1/4 cup cornstarch
1/2 teaspoon salt
4 cups whole milk
2 tablespoons butter
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
M&M's, optional
INSTRUCTIONS
In a heavy saucepan, combine sugar, cocoa, cornstarch and salt. gradually add milk.
Bring to a boil over medium heat; boil and stir for 2 minutes. Remove from the heat; stir in butter and vanilla.
Spoon into individual serving dishes. Chill until serving. Sprinkle with M&M's if desired.
