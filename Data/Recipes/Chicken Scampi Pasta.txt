INGREDIENTS
spaghetti noodles
3 cups shredded chicken
3 cups for whole milk
cilantro
parmesan cheese
5 cups of water
2 cups of bacon chunks
INSTRUCTIONS
Boil the water and insert the noodles when the water begins to vaborate
wait for the noodles to soften
now boil the wilk with the parmesan cheese to give a sauce good texture and taste
cook the bacon to a crisp then create chunks 
cut the cilantro's ends
shred the chicken
add the cooked pasta in the cheese mixture
add the bacon, cilantro, and the chicken
mix well
enjoy!
