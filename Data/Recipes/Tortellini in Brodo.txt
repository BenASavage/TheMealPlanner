INGREDIENTS
2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
2 leeks, halved lengthwise
2 carrots, halved lengthwise
2 small celery stalks
4 cloves garlic
4 cups of low sodium chicken broth
1 small piece parmesan rind
2 wide strips lemon zest
1 12-ounce bone-in smoked pork chop
1 9-ounce package refrigerated cheese or meat tortellini
1 small head escarole
freshly ground pepper
INSTRUCTIONS
Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat
add the leeks, carrots, celery and garlic
cook, stirring occasionally, until the leeks wilt for about 5 minutes
add 2 cups water, thw chicken broth, parmesan rind, lemon zest and pork bone
bring to a simmer and cook until the vegatables are almost tender for about 15 minutes
remove the parmesan rind, lemonzest and pork bone from the pot with a slotted spoon
bring the broth to a boil over medium heat
add the tortellini, escarole and diced pork
season with pepper
cook until the tortellini and escarole are tender for about 4 to 5 minutes
Enjoy!
