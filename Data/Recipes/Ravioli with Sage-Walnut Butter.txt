INGREDIENTS
salt
1/4 cup of balsamic vinegar
2 teaspoons honey
1 bay leaf
2 9-ounce packages of refrigerated cheese ravioli
6 teaspoons unsalted butter
1/3 cup of fresh sage leaves
1 cup walnuts, roughly chopped
3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
INSTRUCTIONS
Boil a large pot of salted water
combine vinegar, honey and bay leaf in a small saucepan
then boil over medium-high heat until syrupy, 4 to 5 minutes
add the ravioli to the boiling water and cook as the label directs
melt the butter in a large skillet over medium heat
add the sage, walnuts and cook until nuts are toasted for about 3 minutes
Drain the ravioli, reserving another 1/2 cup cooking water
add ravioli to the skillet and toss to coat, adding the reserved water as needed
remove from the heat
toss with the parmesan and season with salt
divide the ravioli among plates and drizzle with the balsamic syrup
Enjoy!
